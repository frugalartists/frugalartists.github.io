Project Motto

"Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" is an ancient Sanskrit phrase which means "the world is one family". Unfortunately we are a divided species. Divided by nations, religion, race, ideology, politics, language, gender, and the list goes on and on. We are children of science and technology. We agree with the biology. Genetically, our whole human species is much more similar than most people like to believe. We completely respect an individual's beliefs, no matter how different they are from ours, but then, we also belive, things that divides us are crime against the future of mankind.

Project 1: Unity in Multiplicity

Unity in multiplicity, is our first NFT project with the underlying message of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam". These 729 unique super rare NFTs representing all the communities divided by the biology but united by the love for humanity. PS: All our projects will also have a certain percentage donations dedicated to different charities. Unity in multiplicity will donate 10% of all it's earnings to UNAIDS program.
